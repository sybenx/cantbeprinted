About Can’t Be Printed

Founded by Aaron Herbst, Can’t Be Printed is about empowering you to take control of your financial future through education and Bitcoin. Here’s my story.

My Path to Financial Confidence

In 2017, I saved $12,000 from one of my first jobs, but the housing market was outpacing my efforts. Feeling stuck, I stopped saving and spent freely, unsure of what else to do.

In April 2024, a financial wake-up call led me back to a Bitcoin podcast I’d heard years ago. The enthusiasm of early adopters at a conference, shared by the podcaster, sparked my curiosity. I dove into research, joined a Nostr Bitcoin community, and discovered a video about Witsand, South Africa, where a community embraced Bitcoin as currency. Their confidence inspired me. I bought a small amount, read The Bitcoin Standard, and watched more videos. Each step built my belief in a system that could protect wealth from inflation.

Bitcoin can seem complex, and some shy away from sharing their stories due to privacy concerns. But I believe personal journeys make it relatable. Can’t Be Printed isn’t about pushing Bitcoin—it’s about showing how small steps, like buying a little and learning steadily, gave me financial security. My goal is to help you explore options without feeling overwhelmed.

If you’re uncertain about money, start small. Read, research, and experiment with Bitcoin or other assets. Can’t Be Printed offers knowledge and stories, not sales pitches, to guide you toward confidence in your financial choices.
