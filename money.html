Why Inflation Hurts

Your money buys less every year. Let’s look at why and what you can do.

Why It Happens

Governments print money. 2020-2025: money supply up 38%. Prices rise.

Your Wallet Feels It

$100 in 2020 buys ~$82 today. 2022 inflation hit 9%. Savers lose.

What You Can Do

Here’s how to fight back. Bitcoin’s a strong option—think it through.

Crypto

Bitcoin: Fixed at 21M coins. Grew 4,000% since 2015. Volatile—down 20% in Q1 2025.

Ethereum: Smart contracts, DeFi apps. Same risks: scams, regulations.

Think: High risk, high reward—good for you?

Stocks

S&P 500: ~10% yearly returns. Dropped 20% in 2022. Steady but risky.

Think: Can you wait out market dips?

Bonds

Treasuries: ~3-4% returns. Lagged 9% inflation in 2022. Safe but slow.

Think: Want safety over growth?

Property

Homes: Up ~5% yearly. Costs ~$400K. Hard to sell fast.

Think: Got the cash for this?

Your Move

Inflation won’t stop. Bitcoin’s fixed supply fights it—other options work too. Pick what fits.
